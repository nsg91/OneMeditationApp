## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. The sound is not playing?
Please ensure your device is not in "Silent Mode" (the physical switch on the side of your iPhone) and that your volume is turned up. If you are using Bluetooth headphones, check that they are connected properly.

### 2. How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed directly by Apple.
1. Go to your iPhone **Settings**.
2. Tap your **Name** (Apple ID) at the top.
3. Tap **Subscriptions**.
4. Select **One Meditation** and tap **Cancel Subscription**.

### 3. Can I use the app offline?
Yes! You can download your favorite meditation tracks by tapping the "Download" icon next to the song title. These tracks will be available in your "Offline" tab.
